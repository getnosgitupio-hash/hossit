A second generation owner running 12 outlets across two cities. Her core operating stack: a billing tool at the counter, Excel for daily sales summaries, WhatsApp groups for outlet managers, Tally for accounts, three different vendors for inventory across categories, and a manual roll up every Monday morning that took one full ops salary to compile.

Two prior vendors had quoted ₹47L and ₹62L for the same build. Both proposed 9 to 14 month timelines. She walked away from both.

"I do not want a year of development. I want the Monday morning report to write itself before Diwali."

Two week scoping sprint locked the build at ₹16,87,400 fixed price, 8 week delivery. Live on day 58. Full roll out across all 12 outlets by day 87. By month 6, two of three ops headcount were redeployed to growth.
